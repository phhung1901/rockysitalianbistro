    THE STORY

    Rocky's is a hometown Italian Bistro with both traditional Italian and American classics. Rocky, born in Macedonia, has been perfecting his culinary craft for over 25 years. A true culinary innovator, Rocky takes pride in bringing a taste of Italy to Paris, Illinois.  Whether you are looking for a quick lunch or an elegant date night , The Bistro has an environment and menu for any occasion. 

    ​

    Rocky's Italian Bistro is ran by Rocky and his family. Guests of the Bistro quickly become extended family.  We can't wait for you to try our family recipes for all our home made sauces like Alfredo, Pink Vodka, Tomato Basil and Creamy Pesto!
